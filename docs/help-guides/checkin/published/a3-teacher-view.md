# A3 — `a3-teacher-view`

## The teacher view on your phone

This is the page you pull up when you need to know who's in your room, check a child's alerts, reach a parent, or respond to a parent who's arrived for pickup. It's built to be glanced at quickly.

**Before you start:** check yourself into the room at the kiosk first. This view only shows rooms you're checked into. (Your church also needs to be on Growth tier or above.)

---

### Getting to your room

1. From the dashboard sidebar, tap **Check-In**
2. On the Check-In page, tap the **Teacher View** tile under Quick Actions

You'll land on your rooms.

[SCREENSHOT: a3-checkin-quickactions-with-teacher-view]

If you haven't checked into a room yet, you'll see an empty state pointing you back to the kiosk.

[SCREENSHOT: a3-teacher-view-empty-state]

---

### What the page shows

Each room you're checked into appears as its own section with:

- The room name and total number of children
- A ratio status indicator — **OK**, **Warning**, or **Violation** — based on your church's children-to-adult ratio policy
- The list of children in the room

[SCREENSHOT: a3-teacher-view-with-children]

**Each child's row shows:**

- Name and grade
- An allergy badge if they have one, plus their allergies, medications, and medical notes (your church decides which of these show directly and which you tap to reveal)
- The parent's phone number, masked for privacy as `***1234`
- A **Page parent** button (the coral button on the row) for sending a quick text — see the **B3** guide
- When a parent has arrived: a coral background and ring, a **Parent here for pickup** header, and an **On my way** button — see the **B2** guide
- When attendance is being taken: **Present** and **Not in room** pills below the parent line — see the **B5** guide

---

### Staying current

The page refreshes itself every 30 seconds while it's open. If your phone screen goes to sleep, it pauses to save battery and picks back up when you wake it. You can also pull down to refresh.

---

### Leaving your room

When you're done, check yourself out at the kiosk (or through the usual volunteer checkout). After you check out, this page stops showing your roster.

---

### If something doesn't look right

**"Why is the phone number masked?"** Privacy. You don't need to dial it directly — tap **Page parent** to text them instead.

**"I see a coral row but no buttons."** You may not be checked in. Re-check yourself in at the kiosk.

**"I don't see the room across the hall."** This view only shows rooms you're checked into. Each volunteer sees their own assignments.

---

### Messages you might see

- **"You're not checked in to any rooms"** — head to the kiosk and check in first
- Data looks a few seconds stale — pull down to refresh, or wait for the next 30-second cycle
