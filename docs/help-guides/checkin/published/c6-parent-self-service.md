# C6 — `c6-parent-self-service`

## Managing your pickups and finding your code

**Audience:** Parent / guardian.

**Outcome:** You can manage who's allowed to pick up your child, and find your security code if the text doesn't arrive.

---

### Authorized pickup contacts

From your family pickups page (`/dashboard/account/family/pickups`), you can add an authorized pickup contact or request that one be removed.

[SCREENSHOT: c6-pickups-page]

When you add a contact, every guardian on the household is notified, and the change is recorded in the audit log — so there's always a clear record of who can pick up and when that changed.

[SCREENSHOT: c6-add-pickup-contact]

---

### Finding your security code

If the security-code text doesn't reach you, you can view the code on your phone from your account rather than waiting on the message. Have it ready at the kiosk so the volunteer can match it to your child's name tag.
