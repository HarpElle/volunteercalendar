# C1 — `c1-edit-household`

## Editing a household

**Audience:** Admin or coordinator keeping household records current.

**Outcome:** You can update guardian details, add or remove a child, and change a child's grade or default room.

---

### Editing guardian information

Open the household's detail page and edit the primary or secondary guardian's information — name, phone, and contact details.

[SCREENSHOT: c1-household-edit-modal]

---

### Adding a child

From the household page, add a child and fill in their name, grade, and default room. They'll be available to check in right away.

[SCREENSHOT: c1-child-add-confirm]

---

### Removing a child

Removing a child is a soft delete — the record is hidden rather than erased, so past check-in history stays intact. You'll be asked to confirm before it's removed.

[SCREENSHOT: c1-child-remove-confirm]

---

### Changing grade or default room

Update a child's grade or default room from their entry on the household page. The new room is what the kiosk will auto-assign at the next check-in.
