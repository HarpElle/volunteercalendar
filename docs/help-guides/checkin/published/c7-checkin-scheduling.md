# C7 — `c7-checkin-scheduling`

## Scheduling volunteers for check-in rooms

**Audience:** Admin or coordinator planning who staffs which room.

**Outcome:** Teachers and aides are assigned to specific rooms for a service, so the ratio works out before Sunday.

---

### Assigning a room for a service

For a given service, assign teachers and aides to the rooms they'll cover. Planning assignments ahead of time means each room has enough adults to meet your ratio policy when families start arriving.

[SCREENSHOT: c7-room-assignments]

A scheduled assignment is the plan. The live ratio on Sunday still reflects who has actually **checked into** the room at the kiosk — so remind volunteers that checking in at the kiosk is what makes them count toward the room (see **A3**).

---

### Where this connects

Check-in room assignments use VolunteerCal's broader scheduling tools. For building schedules, handling availability, and sending reminders, see the volunteer scheduling documentation.
