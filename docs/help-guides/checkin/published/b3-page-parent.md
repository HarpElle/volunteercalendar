# B3 — `b3-page-parent`

## Texting a parent from your room

When you need to reach a parent about something that isn't an emergency, **Page parent** sends them a quick text without you leaving the room or dialing a number.

---

### When to use it

- A child is asking for their parent
- A child needs a diaper change and you're out of supplies
- A child is feeling unwell — but not seriously. For anything urgent or medical, find a staff member rather than texting

---

### How it works

Each child's row on the teacher dashboard has a coral **Page parent** button.

[SCREENSHOT: b3-page-parent-button]

Tap it to open a short form with an optional note (up to 200 characters). A note helps the parent know what's going on before they get to you. Useful examples:

- "Aria is asking for you — could you stop by Room 3?"
- "Eli could use a diaper change and we're out of his size."
- "Noah's a little under the weather and would like you."

[SCREENSHOT: b3-page-parent-modal]

---

### Who gets the text

The text goes to the primary guardian and to every authorized contact the parent chose at check-in. If two of those contacts share a phone number, they only get one message.

---

### The 60-second cooldown

After you page a parent, the button pauses for 60 seconds for that child before you can page them again. This keeps a repeated tap from sending a string of texts.

[SCREENSHOT: b3-page-parent-cooldown-state]

---

### What the parent receives

The text identifies your church and room and includes your note. For example:

> Anchor Falls Check-In: A volunteer in Room 3 would like to reach you about Aria. "Aria is asking for you — could you stop by?"

*(Wording shown is an example — confirm the exact message template with your VolunteerCal setup.)*
