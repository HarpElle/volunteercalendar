## Annual grade roll-up

**Audience:** Admin doing the start-of-year update.

**Outcome:** Every child's grade advances to the new school year.

---

### The manual process (current)

Until an automated tool is confirmed, roll grades up in three steps:

1. **Export** your household and child records
2. **Edit** the grade column — advance each child to their new grade, and remove any who have aged out of children's ministry
3. **Import** the updated file back in

Spot-check a handful of children afterward to confirm grades and default rooms came across correctly.

---

### Feature request on file

An automated, one-click grade roll-up — "advance everyone one grade, with a preview and an undo" — would remove the export/edit/import round trip entirely. This is flagged as a future enhancement.
