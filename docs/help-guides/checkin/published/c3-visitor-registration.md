# C3 — `c3-visitor-registration`

## Registering a first-time family at the kiosk

**Audience:** Volunteer at the kiosk, or an admin helping a new family.

**Outcome:** A visiting family is registered and checked in, on the spot, in under a minute.

---

### The flow

1. On the kiosk start screen, tap **New family**
2. Enter the guardian's information — name and phone
3. Add each child — name, grade, and any allergy or medical notes
4. Review the details, then continue into the normal check-in (selecting children, acknowledging any alerts, choosing who can pick up)
